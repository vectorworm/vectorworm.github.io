about BODY JUMPER

2025-02-03

Rex, Charlie, and Sebastian know each other from an underground chatroom, while trying to escape form the increasingly hostile, post-industrial environment. Established by a loose collection of outcasts, this off-grid network allows them to swap bodies and help each other out.
When the network bugs out, they are tasked to investigate. Noone actually knows how the whole system operates, so they have to search into the unknown, through its deepest convolutions. Meanwhile the network seems to develop a mind of its own.
